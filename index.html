Gymnasium & Fitness Centre

SMU’s Gymnasium & Fitness Centre, located at the School of Computing & Information Systems 1, has three levels of facilities: a free weights section at the mezzanine level, and cardio machines & treadmills on the first floor overlooking the SMU Campus Green.

The gym features strength-training equipment, stack-weight machines, kettlebells, and a TRX system to cater to different training purposes and fitness levels.

All users must observe good gym etiquette and follow facility rules for safety and hygiene.

Announcements

[Gym Etiquette] Please observe good gym etiquette:

  • Wipe down equipment after use
  • Return weights to their designated racks
  • Respect others' personal space
  • Use headphones when listening to music

[Gym Attire] Please bring the following items:

  • Sports Attire
  • Water Bottle
  • Towel, show at the counter
  • SMU Card, to exchange for locker key
